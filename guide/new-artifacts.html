Genshin Impact 6.5 New Artifact Set Guide — Full Farming Review

Version 6.5 · ~500 words · Meta

Genshin Impact Version 6.5 introduces updated artifact set balance adjustments alongside improved drop rates for Geo and support-focused artifact sets, greatly benefiting players building the new Lunar Crystallization meta characters. Understanding the best farming routes, set bonuses, and suitable characters allows players to save massive amounts of resin while building optimal endgame gear sets.

Husk of Opulent Dreams — Still Best-in-Slot

The Husk of Opulent Dreams set receives minor balance improvements in 6.5, further solidifying its status as the best Geo defensive DPS artifact set. It provides stacking DEF percentage bonuses and consistent Geo damage increases, perfectly fitting DEF-scaling characters such as Zibai, Linnea, and Noelle. This set remains essential for anyone investing seriously into modern Geo meta team builds.

Lunar Weave — New Support Set

The newly adjusted Lunar Weave support set offers high elemental mastery bonuses and team-wide reaction buffs, designed specifically for support characters who enable crystallization and elemental reaction teams. It works best on Linnea when built as a full support unit, significantly boosting team reaction damage and overall elemental consistency throughout long combat rotations.

Farming Route & Resin Planning

The optimal farming location for these new sets is the Windrise Mountain domain in Mondstadt, which features straightforward enemy layouts and fast clear speeds using standard Geo teams. Players should focus on farming Husk sets primarily for their universal usability, while collecting Lunar Weave pieces as secondary support gear. Proper resin planning allows players to quickly complete new character builds without wasting resources on useless artifact stats.

Genshin Impact 6.5 Spiral Abyss Floor 12 Full Star Guide

Version 6.5 · ~500 words · Meta

Genshin Impact Version 6.5 brings significant changes to Spiral Abyss Floor 12 enemy lineups and floor buffs, requiring updated team setups and strategies for players aiming for full-star clears. This new abyss cycle features specialized reaction bonuses and powerful elite enemies that reward players for using crystallization and normal attack-focused team builds.

First Half — Crystallization Dominance

The first half of Floor 12 receives a massive 75% crystallization damage bonus, making Geo teams extremely dominant in this phase. Enemies include Ruin Grinders, Wild Huntres units, and Moongeckos, which deal high physical damage and require consistent shielding and interruption resistance. The most reliable team for this side is Zibai paired with Linnea, Xingqiu, and Bennett, providing strong shielding, continuous healing, and stacked Geo crystallization damage.

Second Half — Hydro & Hyperbloom Focus

The second half grants a 75% bonus to normal and charged attacks, benefiting hydro and hyperbloom team setups significantly. Players will face Eremite waves, Maguu Kenki, and corrupted beast enemies with high elemental resistance. The Furina and Nahida hyperbloom team remains the best choice here, featuring Nahida for continuous Dendro application, Xingqiu for consistent hydro off-field damage, and Kuki Shinobu for reliable healing and hyperbloom triggers.

F2P Strategy

For fully free-to-play players without limited five-star units, a classic Noelle Geo team works perfectly. Noelle paired with Gorou, Barbara, and Xiangling provides sufficient healing, shielding, and sustained damage to clear every chamber safely. Proper rotation timing and elemental particle management are the key factors for stable full-star runs.

Genshin Impact 6.6 Banner Schedule & Pull Priority Guide

Version 6.6 · ~500 words · Tier List

Genshin Impact Version 6.6 features one of the most anticipated banner lineups of 2026, introducing brand-new five-star characters Nicole and Lorn alongside popular reruns of Dulin and Mavuika. Understanding the exact banner schedule and pull priority helps players allocate primogems wisely and obtain the most meta-relevant characters for endgame progression.

Phase One — Nicole & Dulin (May 20)

Version 6.6 Phase One banners release on May 20, featuring the new five-star Pyro catalyst Nicole as the headline unit alongside the popular Pyro five-star rerun Dulin. The featured four-star character for this phase is Brune, the new Anemo Magecraft support unit essential for completing full Magecraft team synergies. This phase focuses entirely on building the brand-new Magecraft meta core team.

Phase Two — Lorn & Mavuika (June 10)

Phase Two banners launch on June 10, showcasing the new five-star Cryo polearm DPS Lorn and the highly anticipated rerun of Pyro Archon Mavuika. This phase targets players seeking powerful new main DPS units and universal Archon supports capable of fitting into nearly every team type. Both new characters and reruns offer extremely high meta value.

Pull Priority

The official recommended pull priority for version 6.6 is: Nicole first, followed by Dulin, then Lorn, and finally Mavuika. Nicole's core supportive role in the new Magecraft meta makes her the most essential unit for future-proof team building. Dulin offers exceptional off-field Pyro damage, while Lorn delivers top-tier Cryo DPS potential. Mavuika remains a timeless powerful Archon support suitable for all player accounts.

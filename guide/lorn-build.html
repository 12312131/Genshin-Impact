Genshin Impact 6.6 Lorn Build Guide — New Cryo Main DPS

Version 6.6 · ~500 words · Build Guide

Lorn is an upcoming five-star Cryo polearm main DPS arriving in Genshin Impact Version 6.6, designed specifically to dominate the new Magecraft elemental meta system. Boasting high burst damage, strong melt reaction synergy, and unique stacking Magecraft damage buffs, Lorn will quickly establish himself as one of the strongest new main DPS units in the upcoming game version.

Best Artifact Build

The optimal artifact build for Lorn is the four-piece Windblume Ode set, which provides high Cryo damage bonuses, elemental mastery, and reaction damage increases perfectly suited for melt-focused combat rotations. Main stats prioritize ATK% sands, Cryo damage bonus goblet, and critical damage circlet to maximize both burst and sustained combat damage. Substats should focus on critical rate, critical damage, ATK percentage, and elemental mastery for consistent melt reactions.

Best Weapons

Lorn's signature five-star polearm Regret serves as his undisputed best-in-class weapon, offering exclusive Magecraft damage bonuses and high critical damage substats. For players who do not pull the signature weapon, the Primordial Jade Winged-Spear remains an excellent alternative with strong stacking ATK buffs and consistent DPS performance across all team types. Standard four-star polearms such as Deathmatch work well for free-to-play builds.

Team Compositions

His strongest team composition centers on the full Magecraft squad with Nicole, Dulin, and Brune, enabling maximum stacked Magecraft buffs and reliable melt reactions. Players can also pair Lorn with Mavuika and Nicole for aggressive high-risk high-reward melt team setups. With outstanding single-target and AoE damage potential, Lorn will define Cryo DPS meta trends throughout Genshin Impact 6.6.

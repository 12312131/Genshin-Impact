Cryo Polearm Main DPS

Lorn is an upcoming five-star Cryo polearm main DPS arriving in Genshin Impact Version 6.6, designed specifically to dominate the new Magecraft elemental meta system. Boasting high burst damage, strong melt reaction synergy, and unique stacking Magecraft damage buffs, Lorn will quickly establish himself as one of the strongest new main DPS units in the upcoming game version.

Best Artifact Build

The optimal artifact build for Lorn is the four-piece Windblume Ode set, which provides high Cryo damage bonuses, elemental mastery, and reaction damage increases perfectly suited for melt-focused combat rotations.

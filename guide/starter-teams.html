Genshin Impact Best Starter Teams for New Players (2026 Updated)

2026 · ~500 words · F2P

New Genshin Impact players in 2026 benefit greatly from following updated beginner-friendly team builds optimized for fast early-game progression, easy resource farming, and comfortable world exploration. Proper starter team selection drastically reduces early-game difficulty and allows faster spiral abyss progression for new accounts.

Absolute Beginner Team (Free Characters Only)

The absolute beginner starting team uses only free starter characters obtained immediately after creating an account. Amber provides Pyro application for elemental reactions, Kaeya delivers consistent Cryo damage and freeze utility, Lisa offers powerful early Electro AoE damage, and the Anemo Traveler supplies crowd control and elemental swirl reactions. This team handles all early world content without additional pulls or investments.

Best Early-Game F2P Team

The best early-game F2P team for progressing players features Xiangling, Bennett, Xingqiu, and Sucrose. These four-star units are easily obtainable through standard wishes and beginner event rewards, forming the classic national team capable of clearing most mid-game content and early spiral abyss floors. This balanced team offers healing, ATK buffs, off-field damage, and crowd control.

First Five-Star Integration

For players lucky enough to obtain their first five-star character early, simply replace one four-star unit with the new five-star while keeping Bennett and Xingqiu as core supports. These two four-star supports provide enough utility and damage buffs to make nearly any five-star character perform extremely well. Mastering basic rotation mechanics with beginner-friendly teams creates a solid foundation for future advanced meta team building.

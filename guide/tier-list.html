Genshin Impact 6.5 Character Tier List — 2026 Updated Meta

Version 6.5 · ~500 words · Tier List

Genshin Impact Version 6.5 introduces major meta shifts centered around the new Lunar Crystallization reaction, reshaping the overall character tier list for spiral abyss, overworld exploration, and event content. Many traditional top-tier units remain strong, while newly released characters instantly dominate the highest rankings due to their unique kit mechanics and team buff capabilities.

SS-Tier

SS-tier characters in version 6.5 include Zibai and Linnea as the new core Geo meta duo. Zibai offers unparalleled sustained Geo DPS with DEF scaling damage, while Linnea provides team-wide resistance reduction, healing, and crystallization amplification. Furina and Nahida maintain permanent SS-tier positions due to their universal team compatibility and unmatched off-field elemental application and damage buffs for almost every team type in the game.

S-Tier

S-tier characters consist of classic meta staples including Neuvillette, Kaedehara Kazuha, Yelan, Hu Tao, and Ayaka. These units still deliver excellent performance across all content types and remain safe investments for both new players and veterans. Kazuha's crowd control and elemental buffs, alongside Yelan's off-field hydro damage and speed bonuses, keep them essential in nearly every competitive team lineup.

Dominant Team Archetypes

Current dominant meta team builds include the Lunar Crystallization Geo team, hyperbloom Dendro team, and traditional freeze teams. The new Geo meta currently outperforms most older builds in spiral abyss due to version-specific crystallization buffs. Free-to-play players should prioritize building four-star national team units such as Bennett, Xingqiu, and Xiangling, as these timeless supports continue to perform reliably regardless of meta changes.

Genshin Impact 6.5 New Weapon Guide — Best Options for New Characters

Version 6.5 · ~500 words · Build Guide

Version 6.5 delivers several new weapon additions and balance tweaks that perfectly match the kit mechanics of newly released characters Zibai and Linnea. Understanding weapon rankings, F2P alternatives, and niche use cases helps players maximize damage output without spending primogems on unnecessary weapon banner pulls.

Lunar Blade — Zibai's Best-in-Slot

Lunar Blade, the new five-star Geo sword introduced in 6.5, serves as Zibai's undisputed best-in-class weapon. It provides stacking DEF conversion damage bonuses, increased Geo damage, and improved shield strength, fully synergizing with his core kit mechanics and Lunar Crystallization team playstyle. This weapon significantly increases Zibai's sustained damage compared to older sword options.

Linnea's Polearm Options

For Linnea players, the Favonius Lance remains the best free-to-play polearm choice due to its high energy recharge stat and consistent elemental particle generation. Reliable burst uptime is critical for Linnea's supportive role, allowing continuous team buffs, healing, and resistance reduction throughout combat. The Prototype Starglitter also works well as a secondary F2P alternative with balanced stats.

Premium & Legacy Options

Premium players can use Freedom-Sworn for Linnea, granting powerful team elemental mastery and reaction damage buffs that elevate entire team performance. Older staple weapons such as Summit Shaper and Whiteblind still maintain excellent usability for Geo DPS characters. Overall, version 6.5 weapon changes greatly improve the quality of life for Geo team builders, offering both premium and accessible free options.

Geo Polearm Support Sub-DPS

Linnea is a brand-new five-star Geo support and sub-DPS character introduced in Genshin Impact Version 6.5, quickly becoming a core meta unit for crystallization-focused team compositions. Designed for defensive support, resistance shredding, team healing and DEF-based damage amplification, she greatly improves the stability and overall damage output of Geo teams in both spiral abyss and open-world content.

Best Artifact Builds

For artifact builds, the four-piece Husk of Opulent Dreams set remains her best-in-class option. This set provides massive DEF percentage bonuses and consistent Geo damage buffs that perfectly match Linnea's scaling mechanics. For free-to-play players, a mixed two-piece Husk plus two-piece Tenacity of the Millelith build works wonderfully, offering balanced defense and team ATK buffs.

Linnea Build Guide — Best Artifacts, Weapons & Teams

Version 6.5 · ~500 words · Build Guide

Linnea is a brand-new five-star Geo support and sub-DPS character introduced in Genshin Impact Version 6.5, quickly becoming a core meta unit for crystallization-focused team compositions. Designed for defensive support, resistance shredding, team healing and DEF-based damage amplification, she greatly improves the stability and overall damage output of Geo teams in both spiral abyss and open-world content.

Best Artifact Builds

For artifact builds, the four-piece Husk of Opulent Dreams set remains her best-in-class option. This set provides massive DEF percentage bonuses and consistent Geo damage buffs that perfectly match Linnea's scaling mechanics. For free-to-play players, a mixed two-piece Husk plus two-piece Tenacity of the Millelith build works wonderfully, offering balanced defense and team ATK buffs.

Recommended main stats are DEF% sands, Geo damage bonus goblet, and either DEF% or critical rate circlet. Substats should prioritize DEF percentage, critical stats, energy recharge, and elemental mastery in that order.

Best Weapons

In terms of weapon choices, Freedom-Sworn is her signature premium option, providing team-wide buffs and elemental mastery. For F2P users, the Favonius Lance is highly recommended due to its reliable energy recharge, allowing consistent elemental burst rotation. The Prototype Starglitter also serves as a solid alternative for players who lack five-star polearms.

Best Team Compositions

When building teams, Linnea fits perfectly into the new Lunar Crystallization meta. Her strongest lineup pairs her with Zibai, Columbina, and Furina for high-end spiral abyss clearing. Casual players can use a budget combination featuring Zhongli, Gorou, and Ningguang for a pure Geo setup. She also works well with Navia and standard F2P supports, making her one of the most flexible and accessible new supports in Genshin Impact 6.5.

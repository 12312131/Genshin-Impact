Genshin Impact 6.6 Magecraft System Complete Guide

Version 6.6 · ~500 words · Meta

The Magecraft system is a brand-new elemental mechanic launching in Genshin Impact Version 6.6, introducing exclusive team buffs, specialized reaction bonuses, and unique character synergies for newly released 6.6 units. This new meta system completely reshapes endgame team building and creates entirely new dominant combat strategies for spiral abyss and event content.

How Magecraft Works

The Magecraft mechanic activates when multiple designated Magecraft characters are included in the same team composition. Each Magecraft unit provides unique passive buffs that stack significantly when paired together, boosting overall team attack damage, elemental reaction power, and defensive capabilities. The core four Magecraft units consist of Nicole, Lorn, Dulin, and Brune, each offering distinct supportive or offensive Magecraft bonuses.

Core Team Roles

Nicole acts as the foundational Magecraft support, generating strong shields and providing universal ATK buffs for all Magecraft team members. Her presence drastically improves team survivability and overall damage output, making her mandatory for maximum Magecraft team efficiency. Lorn serves as the primary Magecraft DPS dealer, gaining massive reaction damage bonuses when supported by other Magecraft teammates.

Full Squad Synergy

Dulin provides off-field Pyro application for consistent melt and vaporize reactions, while Brune adds crowd control and elemental mastery buffs to complete the full Magecraft squad. The fully stacked four-person Magecraft team delivers unprecedented damage potential in version 6.6 meta. Players preparing for the new version should save resources to build these units early and dominate the upcoming spiral abyss meta shift.

Zibai Build Guide — Best Teams & Playstyle

Version 6.5 · ~500 words · Build Guide

Zibai is the flagship five-star Geo main DPS released in Genshin Impact 6.5, serving as the primary damage dealer for the brand-new Lunar Crystallization reaction meta. With unique Geo scaling mechanics, high sustained damage, and strong synergy with Linnea, Zibai has quickly risen to SS-tier status in both spiral abyss rankings and regular gameplay content.

Best Artifact Builds

The best artifact set for Zibai is the four-piece Husk of Opulent Dreams, which maximizes his DEF conversion damage bonus. Players who prefer more defensive consistency can also use a four-piece Retracing Bolide build for shield strength and boosted charged attacks. Main stats should focus on DEF% sands, Geo damage goblet, and critical damage circlet to maintain high burst and sustained output.

Best Weapons

For weapons, the Summit Shaper five-star sword is Zibai's best-in-class choice, offering ATK and shield-based damage bonuses that greatly enhance his overall performance. Free-to-play players can safely use the Whiteblind craftable sword, which stacks DEF and ATK bonuses perfectly suited for his kit. No other four-star weapon provides comparable consistency for F2P users.

Team Compositions & Rotation

In team composition, his strongest meta team consists of Zibai paired with Linnea, Columbina, and Yelan, delivering extremely fast spiral abyss floor 12 clears. For budget-friendly setups, players can replace limited five-stars with Xingqiu and Bennett, creating a reliable F2P-friendly variant. The standard rotation starts with Columbina elemental skill, followed by Linnea buffs, Yelan burst, and finally Zibai elemental burst and hold elemental skill for maximum damage stacking.

Genshin Impact 6.6 Nicole Build Guide — Upcoming 5-Star Pyro Support

Version 6.6 · ~500 words · Build Guide

Nicole is an upcoming five-star Pyro catalyst character scheduled for release in Genshin Impact Version 6.6, serving as the core support unit for the brand-new Magecraft meta system. Featuring powerful shield generation, massive team ATK buffs, and unique Magecraft synergy mechanics, Nicole will become an essential support for multiple upcoming version 6.6 meta team compositions.

Best Artifact Build

The best artifact set for Nicole is the brand-new Heavenly Grace four-piece set designed exclusively for Magecraft system characters. This set provides high ATK percentage bonuses, shield strength increases, and specialized Magecraft reaction buffs that drastically improve team damage output. Recommended main stats include ATK% sands, ATK% goblet, and ATK% circlet for maximum supportive buff power. Substats should prioritize ATK percentage, energy recharge, and elemental mastery.

Best Weapons

For weapon choices, Light of Seven Dawns, Nicole's signature five-star catalyst, delivers perfect stat distribution and exclusive Magecraft team bonuses, making it her best-in-class option. Players without the signature weapon can use the Skyward Atlas for high base ATK and consistent elemental damage buffs. The Widsith four-star catalyst also works as a strong free-to-play alternative with flexible combat buffs.

Team Building

In team building, Nicole leads the core Magecraft lineup alongside Lorn, Dulin, and Brune, creating a fully synergized elemental team with massive reaction damage bonuses. She also fits universally into traditional meta teams featuring Furina and Nahida, providing additional shielding and ATK buffs for standard hyperbloom and reaction builds. Nicole's flexible kit ensures she will remain a top-tier support long after version 6.6 launches.
